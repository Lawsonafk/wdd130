Accra, Ghana

Accra is the capital city of Ghana. It houses the seat of govenment and other salient institutions from education to health, and many more. The natives of tis city before the arrival of the Europeans solely depends on fishing and farming activities for their survival. When it comes to the domain of trading, they practiced the trading system called bater-trading which simply implies the exchange of goods for good.
