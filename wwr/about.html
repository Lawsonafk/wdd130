History

Apex Retreat Rafting was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who shared a deep love for adventure and nature. The company's journey began as a humble rafting operation on a small river, but quickly gained a reputation for providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences. In its early years, It is focused on building a team of experienced guides and acquiring top-notch equipment to ensure the highest level of safety and customer satisfaction. The company's commitment to excellence and dedication to preserving the natural environment became the foundation of its success. As the years went by, Natural Retreat Rafting expanded its operations to include rafting trips on various rivers across the country.
